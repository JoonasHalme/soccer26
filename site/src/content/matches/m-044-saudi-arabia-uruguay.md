Group H's other Day 5 opener at Hard Rock Stadium. Late afternoon kickoff in Miami, neutral venue.

**Saudi Arabia** are the most-invested-in Asian football program of the past five years. The squad benefits from the Saudi Pro League salary boom (familiarity with high-level training environments) but also from the Saudi Pro League's lower competitive ceiling than top European leagues. Elo (1579) reflects an inconsistent run since their 2022 World Cup high (the famous Argentina win). Capable of an upset; not a tier-1 side.

**Uruguay** are the South American tournament veteran. Through generational handover from the Suárez/Cavani era to the Núñez/Pellistri/Araujo generation, with central spine in De La Cruz and Valverde. Elo (1684) is fair. Tactically intense, vertical, transitions through pace; classic Uruguay grit on set pieces.

**Model view.** Uruguay **19% / 24% / 57%**, xG **0.86–1.69**, BTTS 47%. Clean Uruguay lean.

**Betting angle.** The market will price this in the 1.65–1.75 range on Uruguay ML, which is roughly fair on the model. Where to find edge:

- **Uruguay -0.5 AH** at 1.65+ — this is just ML in disguise but sometimes priced better
- **Uruguay -1.5 AH** at 2.30+ — model implies ~35%, fair ~2.86, books often miss on the spread
- **Saudi Arabia +1.5 AH** at 1.95+ — model implies ~76%, fair ~1.32 — sometimes available at value if books haven't tuned the line

Avoid: BTTS markets (basically a coin flip, no edge), draw at short prices.
