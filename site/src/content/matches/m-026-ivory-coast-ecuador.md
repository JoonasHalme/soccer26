They are a balanced 4-3-3 with strong wide attacking play, a midfield destroyer, and one of the more underrated GK situations of the tournament. Elo (1736) is at the top end of what they've ever rated; the AFCON win is doing meaningful work.

The current squad blends the Caicedo/Sarmiento generation with veterans like Estupiñán at fullback. Style is a compact mid-block, low possession, transitions through the front three. Elo (1702) is fair.
